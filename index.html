About Another Blog Blog

Another Blog Blog is here to bring together the best in current events reporting, creative writing and independent music.

ABB was originally created as a space for Mr. Matt Brompton to practice his coding. During GE2017, despite being a Labour Party member Matt couldn't decide who to vote for. Once the Labour Manifesto was leaked he decided to research the issues raised while forming his own opinion as to whether or not they were viable.

So, using the platform he had available and the domain he bought (just to learn how); Matt began to write between working a 50 hour per week farm job.

Although Mr. Brompton is still the main creator and coder for the site 3 other people are soon to come on-board and in this process ABB will grow into a platform for investigative blogging. We will endeavour to combat fake news despite its source by working to our 'responsible reporting' policy.

So stick with us and expand your mind while learning more about current events, finding the best in independent music and creative writing.
