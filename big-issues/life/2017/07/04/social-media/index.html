Social Media: Gift or Curse?

DrayZera

People are depressed even more, But… Do you know the social score? Well, we can’t necessarily put a finger on it, Yet the low lit screens flickers and suffocates us… Oh social media… Is killing us all!

Welcome ONE AND ALL to my debut article on Another Blog Blog! My name is DrayZera and while I chomp on my sausage rolls en route from Liverpool Lime Street to Shoreham-On-Sea near Brighton, I’d like to login to my thoughts in regards to social media (see what I did here) and that it’s killing us all! Here is why…

As a person born in the earliest of 90s, I grew up away from the computer world and by the time I hit my teens I recall using a floppy-disk to save my work from Microsoft Power-point!

OH HOW THE WORLD HAS CHANGED!

Here are some stats:

"84% of all UK adults use social media... YouTube and Facebook are by far the most popular platforms among UK adults online (respectively 85% and 78%)"Stats from the We Are Flint Report

Due to social media - hashtags, selfies, filters and memes all are prevalent and are a part of society’s idiolect lingo! Now, while new language is always a fresh welcome to the ever-bulging Oxford Dictionary, it’s important to know the ABSOLUTE SPEED this is happening… Within the last 5-10 years!

"In March 2007, less than a year after opening to the public, the social network had 30 million users." - betanews

And with time flying… So does the frazzle effect it inflicts upon the human mind.

It has caused infection and poison upon the innocent in that your mind has to CONTINUE to look at Facebook, BURROW YOUR MIND unto those absolute terrible videos from Buzzers and so on! We are hooked and we are addicted.

Now, DISCLAIMER, I’m not saying that social media in itself is bad at all! Quite the contrary! The tools that social media has in abundance have allowed yours truly to spread his performer name worldwide and has assisted my lifestyle muchly! However, the way we are applying social media is killing us without remorse… So here’s the cold hard discourse I’d like to share:

Talking behind a screen is easier than talking in front of one another - Bullying on the Internet is vile especially when you see comments such as “kys” (an acronym for Kill Yourself) and so on! Leads to depression, anxiety and with lots of pain mentally, physically and emotionally. Not many likes to confront face to face…

Spending too much time in the electronic world compared to the REAL World - You get games such as “World Of Warcraft” “Second Life” and Twich Livestreaming which incorporates a heavy Social Media aspect and therefore neglecting the beautiful nature of skylines, beaches and roller coasters! Sleep deprivation and the feeling of “Wasting your life away” is there! Now, if you’re a pro gamer and making money then that’s cool! However, the vast majority of us don’t and while money isn’t everything… Spending hours not investing in yourself in reality isn’t right!

Are social skills deminishing?

I have realised in my months of travelling far and wide that people’s social skills have deminished greatly in comparison to the older generation! By not practicing social skills person to person has caused us to become more of a hermit and therefore has become quite the factor in developing phobias with people as a consequence!

SO with all that said… What’s the solution? Simple. Social Media less, go out more! DrayZera
