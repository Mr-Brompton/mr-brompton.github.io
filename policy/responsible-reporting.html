Another Blog Blog Responsible Reporting Policy.

Another Blog Blog(ABB) is commited to responsible reporting. In order to ensure that all content brought to you is of the highest quality all posts as of 25/06/2017 will abide by our responsible reporting policy as laid out below.

  1. ABB is commited to truth and accuracy. Although ABB cannot garuantee "truth" we will always strive for accuracy.

  2. Where we cannot corroberate sources or ascertain "truth" it will be plainly stated.

  3. All facts and figures used in Another Blog Blog will be sourced, cross checked and linked against reliable sources where possible.

  4. Our reliable sources include Fullfact.org, IFS.org.uk as well as other recognised institutes.

  5. Where possible we will use comments from experts in their field. These will be marked as such and linked to the original source.

  6. All writers of ABB are independent artists and creators. ABB cannot accept responsibility or liability for opinion. Where opinion is published it will be clearly marked.

  7. We will not publish information that could cause harm to individual, in particular in the case of ongoing legal cases and defemation of character. This does not include harm to an individuals reputation where the information is proven to be true.

  8. ABB will be aware of any potential harm that may be caused through our words.

  9. ABB holds itself accountable to its readers. Readers may challenge content through our contact links or social media pages. We will endevour to investigate and respond to all concerns.

  10. Sometimes false information may be published despite our best efforts. In this case we will correct the information and publish a correction statement as appropriate.
